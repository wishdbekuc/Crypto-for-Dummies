What is Crypto?
Cryptocurrency, simply put, is a form of digital money. The main difference between Cryptocurrency and fiat (normal) money is that Cryptocurrency is backed by a different kind of technology - blockchains. Blockchains enable Cryptocurrencies to change the way we transact, potentially providing cheaper, more durable, trust-less and faster financial services to everyone. Although not mature, Cryptocurrencies are excepted to disrupt a multitude of industries such as finance and law.
